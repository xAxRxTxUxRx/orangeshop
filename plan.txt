PRODUCT (class)
- id (long)
- category (enum) (смесители, мебель, душевая программа, аксессуары)
- collection (enum) (...)
- images (list<img>)
- title (str)
- price (embeded class)
  - fixed_price (float)
  - discount (float)	
  - delivey_price (float)
- kit (list<str>)
- description (str)
- advantages (list<pair<img, str>>)
- specifications (list<pair<str, str>>)
- assembly_diagram (img)
* cart (cart)

CART (class)
- id (long)
- fixed_price (float)
- discount (float)
- promocode (boolean)	
- delivey_price (float)
- nds (float)
- final_price (float)
* products (list<product>)

- order_state (enum) (...)
